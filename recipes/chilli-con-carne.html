The Flavorful Chilli Con Carne

Description

Chilli Con Carne is a classic family favourite recipe. Here's my version, tried and tested over 25 years. It's great for batch cooking or an easy midweek dinner. Packed with minced beef and red kidney beans, make it mild… or go hot!

It's the perfect summer entree sure to please your whole family with just the right amount of spice!

Chilli Con Carne Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Onion and garlic
  • Mild chilli powder
  • Sweet smoked paprika
  • Ground cumin
  • Dried oregano
  • Dark brown sugar
  • Tinned chopped tomatoes
  • Beef stock
  • Red kidney beans
  • Dark chocolate

Steps

How to make Chilli Con Carne

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and brown the mince.
  2. Add the onion and spices and cook.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer
  4. Remove from the heat and stir in the chocolate until it's melted

In the fridge You can keep this recipe in the fridge for 3 days. Reheat fully in the microwave or hob before serving.

In the freezer This is a great recipe for batch cooking for freezing. Just wait until it’s totally cold and then put into a freezer proof container and freeze for up to 3 months. Defrost thoroughly before reheating in the microwave or hob.
